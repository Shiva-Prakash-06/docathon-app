About DOCathon

All it takes is all you've got

OVERVIEW

DOCATHON is the central sports body of the Department of Commerce, focusing on organizing intra-class and inter-department sports events that build team spirit, leadership, and a culture of unity among students. With the 2025–2026 edition, DOCATHON is committed to building not just athletes but a community where physical fitness, mental wellness, and social responsibility merge into a transformative student experience.


Our mission
We aim to foster a vibrant sports culture that builds leadership, team spirit, sportsmanship, and unity across classes.


Our vision
To cultivate a cohesive community where every student feels empowered to engage in sports, promoting not only physical well-being but also camaraderie and a sense of belonging within the department.

Our Theme

MAIN THEME: MENTAL HEALTH AWARENESS

Sport-wise Themes
  • Cricket → Depression & Anxiety
  • Football → Suicide Prevention
  • Basketball → Substance Abuse & Addiction
  • Throwball → Traumatic Brain Injury (TBI)
  • Volleyball → PTSD & Trauma
  • Table Tennis → OCD Awareness (Repetition & Cycles)
  • Chess → Eating Disorders & Body Image (PCOD & PCOS)
  • Carrom Board → Loneliness & Social Isolation
